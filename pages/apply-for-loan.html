Empowerment Loans Initiative

The Youth Spring Empowerment Loans Initiative provides interest-free, small-scale startup loans to youth and underprivileged individuals with viable ideas. This program aims to foster entrepreneurship and financial independence among Nigerian youth.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • You must be a Nigerian youth or an underprivileged individual.
  • You need to present a viable business idea with a clear plan for success.
  • Applicants must be able to provide a reliable guarantor.

Loan Details:

  • Amount: ₦50,000 - ₦500,000
  • Interest-Free Loan
  • Repayment Period: 12 months
  • Mentorship during and after disbursement
